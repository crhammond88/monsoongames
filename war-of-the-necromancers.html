War of the Necromancers is a fast-paced, single-player battle arena style game for web browsers. MOBA players should find the gameplay very familiar, and players new to the genre can learn the basics of MOBA gameplay without being overwhelmed by complex item systems and team dynamics.

WotN is currently in Open Beta.
