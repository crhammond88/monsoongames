Monsoon Games is owned and operated by Chris Hammond. Currently, the company has no employees.

War of the Necromancers is the first title released by Monsoon Games and was developed exclusively by Chris. The game was produced with the Unity3D game engine on a non-existent budget using a combination of free-licensed assets and custom creations.
